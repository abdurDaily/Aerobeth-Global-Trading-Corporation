About Aerobeth

Your Trusted Partner for Industrial Growth

Founded in 2019, Aerobeth Global Trading Corporation has grown into a leading provider of turnkey solutions and export-import services. Headquartered at 10/2 Gawsia Kashem Tower, Arambagh, Motijheel, Dhaka–1000, with a branch at Ward No. 09, Mazer Fadi Station, Chakaria, Cox's Bazar – 4741.

Our core services — Turnkey Solutions and Export-Import — are delivered with uncompromising quality, technical precision, and full accountability. We work with verified global partners in China, Hong Kong, and beyond to bring the best industrial products and solutions to our clients.

  • End-to-end turnkey project execution with zero compromise
  • Verified global supplier network — China, Hong Kong & beyond
  • Quality-driven procurement for industrial & consumer sectors
  • Business hours: Saturday–Thursday, 09:00–17:00 BST
